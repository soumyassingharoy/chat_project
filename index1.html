  • OVERVIEW

    Built across more than 4 acres of land and situated in the Industrial and Business Park – I, Godrej Eternia is Chandigarh’s first modern commercial complex with ready to move office space/ retail space. Central to the tri-city of Chandigarh, Mohali and Panchkula, this property is close to many upcoming international hotels, malls and showrooms.

    Godrej Eternia has been designed by world renowned architects HBRA (Hammond Beeby Rupert Ainge, USA) and Patel Batliwala & Associates. The office spaces here come with flexible floor plans which offer seamless scope for expansion, with an option of exclusive penthouses on the terrace floor. The entire building has contemporary French windows to provide soothing views of the lush greenery all around. This commercial complex is ideal for high-end offices, restaurants, health centers and salons. Sale Price start from –Rs. 55 Lakhs onwards.
